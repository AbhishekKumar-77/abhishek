The old forts, desert oceans, and enthralling cultural beauty of Rajasthan have made Jaisalmer, one of the best tourist attractions in India. From discovering its art and architecture to the safari in the desert, Jaisalmer offers a memorable vacation.

f you are looking for a holiday destination in November or December, Jaisalmer is the right place to visit. Summers here can be unbearable, while the winter season the best time for a desert tour in India. There are fort tours and adventure activities for you while the traditional dance and food is something that you must enjoy.

Best Time To Visit: October to February How To Reach: Jodhpur airport is the nearest domestic airport to Jaisalmer, which is approximately 285 km from the city. There are many buses operating from Jodhpur and other cities to Jaisalmer. To reach Jodhpur airport, you can board a flight to Indira Gandi international airport in Delhi. Ideal Duration: 3-4 days Nearest Airport: Jodhpur Airport Places to Visit: Jaisalmer Fort, Kuldhara Village, Gadisar Lake, Desert National Park, Badal Palace, Patwon ki Haveli Things to Do: Fort Tours, Desert Safari, Camel Ride, Dune Bashing, Dance and Music Show, Sand Boarding Best Places to Stay: Sultan Resorts, Hotel Rawalkot Jaisalmer, Jaisalmer Marriott Resort & Spa, Suryagarh Jaisalmer
